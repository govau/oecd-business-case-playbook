<p>Our website aims to meet the Australian Government’s web accessibility requirements. These include the World Wide Web Consortium’s <a href="http://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG20/" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow">Web Content Accessibility Guidelines version 2.0 (WCAG 2.0)</a> at level AA.</p>
<p>We want to provide our information for everyone, including people with a disability.</p>
<p>Please get in touch with us if anything on this site is inaccessible to you or you experience problems accessing our content.</p>
